WEB DEVELOPMENT

Starting in 2018, I was extremely intrested in coding in general. I talked to a few people who knew more then I did. In the end I was recomended to learn Python. As a result, I watched a 7 hour video that worked on teaching every small aspect of basic Python. I finished this class and got nowhere. I had no idea what to do with what I had learned.



With only basic background knowledge, I signed up for a online self paced online course over the summer. While working through this class I also worked with my neighbor, who is a software developer, to start to get ahead. When I realised I don't even have a use for the analytical power of Python, I dropped it.



This year I began teaching myself HTML and CSS with the help of W3SCHOOLS.COM and CODEACADEMY.COM. This is the product of my progress. I learn best when doing instead of listening or writing. This is me taking advantage of knowing my strengths.
